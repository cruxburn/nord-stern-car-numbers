3-Year Rolling Period System
How It Works
  • Car numbers are reserved for 3 years from the reservation date
  • If used within the 3-year period, the reservation extends for another 3 years
  • Usage is tracked and automatically renews the expiration date
  • Unused numbers expire after 3 years and become available
